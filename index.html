About Me

I am Co-founder and Head of AI at Vijil, a technology startup on a mission to help enterprises build and operate trustworthy AI agents. I am also a founding Member of the Board of Directors at AI Risk and Vulnerability Alliance, a US nonprofit in the area of AI risk. Over the past years, I have been an applied researcher in multiple companies. Most notably, I was a senior scientist in the security research team at Splunk and the Data Science and AI Research team at AT&T Labs.

My research is on holistic and practical trustworthy AI, aimed at proposing novel solutions to technical problems in the reliability, safety, and security of AI, that are motivated by and readily applicable to real-world applications. I have pioneered the use of trustworthy AI methods in multiple companies, written a book, and founded multiple nonprofit efforts in this area. My research in the data for good space has driven policy changes in government and nonprofit organizations, such as the UNICEF Office of Innovation and Chicago Department of Public Health. My work has received global recognition through outlets such as MIT Tech Review, Orange UK, the Turing Podcast, and Chicago Tribune. I am a recipient of the Early Career Award in Statistics and Data Sciences by the International Indian Statistical Association.

I received my PhD in Statistics from The University of Minnesota Twin Cities. I hold bachelors and masters degrees in Statistics from Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata.

Please see my CV for more details on my work.
