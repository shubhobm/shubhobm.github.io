Research interests

Multivariate quantiles and statistical depth functions, depth-based inference;

Robust dimension reduction and variable selection, application in genetic data;

In 2016-17 I will be collaborating with Prof. Saonli Basu of the Division of Biostatistics and Minnesota Center of Twin and Family Research on devising and implementing strategies for selecting important genetic and environmental factors behind the development of behavioral traits, by analyzing data on twins and families.

Applications in climate modelling;

Since Jan 2015 I have been working as a research assistant in the $10M National Science Foundation project on developing data-driven methods to understand climate change.

Predictive modelling in statistical chemistry;

Collaborator Prof. Subhash C. Basak.
