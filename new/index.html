About Me

I am a senior scientist in the Data Science and AI Research team of AT&T Chief Data Office (erstwhile part of AT&T Bell Labs). during my time in AT&T, I have worked on a variety of business problems Currently, my focus is on trustworthy machine learning in the wild: not only proposing novel solutions to problems that ensure qualities such as fairness, transparency, privacy, and robustness, but also implementing them in diverse, real-world use cases.

I deeply believe in the power of data to bring about positive changes in the world, and taking a participatory approach towards achieving these changes. Recently, I co-founded the Trustworthy ML Initiative as an effort to build a community and facilitating dialogue among researchers and practitioners in this field.

Before joining AT&T, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at University of Florida Informatics Institute. I received my PhD in Statistics from The University of Minnesota Twin Cities.
